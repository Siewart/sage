# Summaries for Competitive Age of Empires 2

For this scientific research we are looking for competitive Age of Empires 2 viewers, casters and players to investigate the requirements and needs for summaries for competitive Age of Empires 2 games and propose a solution. The study will be conducted through Microsoft Forms on March 5 through 15.  
Participation takes about 15 minutes.  
As a subject in the research, you will be confronted with one or several videos summarizing a previously played game, followed by questions regarding the events of the video. The questions are aimed to record your opinion on the relevance of the content and the quality of the format and the style used to bring this content. In addition, you are asked to report your level of experience with the game.  
The answers might be used to verify the quality of the videos and the systems behind it, to make further improvements. Additionally, we use your anonymous data for automation purposes, including be not limited to "Machine Learning" and summarization for reporting. This data will be handled by the researchers of this research. After ensuring all data’s anonymity the data might be made available publicly. As a result, the data will be kept indefinitely.  
Due to the unidentifiable nature of data you provide, you cannot withdraw you data after submitting. To withdraw simply close the browser window without submitting. We will however completely anonymize or remove any data you may provide in open fields to ensure your anonymity where needed – you are however discouraged from entering any personally identifiable information in the form.
