# 1001 Tales of Competitive Age of Empires 2

In this experiment you are invited to share your opinion on live match updates for competitive Age of Empires 2 matches.

## Purpose:

- Targeting viewers, casters, and players
- Understand requirements for **live match updates** texts
- Explore the needs for **casting** and **match summaries**
- Conducted between **Aug 26 – Sep 2**

## Participation:

- Duration: **±20 minutes**
- Measure **quality** of **different texts**

## Anonymity:

- Responses handled only by researchers.
- Data may be made public **anonymously**.
- Avoid entering personal info.

## Withdrawal:

- Close browser without submitting to withdraw; partial responses deleted.
- After submission, withdrawal allowed **within 7 days**.
- Optional email for post-study withdrawal contact, email will be deleted after 7 days.
